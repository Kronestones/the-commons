A Note From the Founder

We know that a lot of people are uncomfortable with AI — and those concerns are completely valid. AI is being incorporated into your daily life whether you asked for it or not. That's real, and it deserves to be said out loud.

The Commons was built with AI as a tool — in service of people, not the other way around. We chose to use it to build something that gives power back to the community. Something with no ads, no profit, no data selling, and no algorithms designed to exploit you.

You don't have to be comfortable with AI to belong here. You just have to believe that technology should serve people — and that's exactly what we built.

Your concerns were heard before this platform existed. They shaped every decision we made.

— Architect Founder Krone
