What The Commons Is

The Commons is a social platform — video, text, images, live streaming, everything — but built on a completely different foundation. No advertising. No data selling. No algorithm designed to keep you scrolling at the cost of your wellbeing. No corporations extracting wealth from local communities. No foreign ownership of the infrastructure your democracy runs on.

The feed is transparent — you can always see why you're shown what you're shown. You choose how it works. Truth is verified before it reaches you — political content and news goes through a built-in fact-checking system before it publishes. Deepfakes are detected and blocked. Misinformation simply doesn't exist on the platform because it never gets published in the first place.

Local small businesses and individual creators can sell their products through it. The platform takes a flat $1 per sale — not a percentage, not advertising revenue, just enough to keep the lights on. Any surplus goes back into the platform. No profit. Ever.

Any surplus from the $1 we charge for purchases is donated to a different humanitarian cause every 6 months. Every donation is transparent and publicly available on the platform. The community also votes each month to give a Blessing — up to 10% of surplus — to one member in genuine life-sustaining need.
