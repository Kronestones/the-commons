The Monthly Blessing 💛

One person. One month. Community chosen.

Each month, The Commons community selects one member in genuine life-sustaining need to receive a Blessing. The team verifies need. The community votes. One person or family receives the Blessing.

Medical. Housing. Food security. Genuine life-sustaining need only. Not wants.

Maximum $19,000 for individuals. $38,000 for families. Every dollar transparent. The Commons is a facilitator only.
