Your device is broken and you don't know why. Maybe it's slow. Maybe it crashed. Maybe something just feels wrong.

Kinto can diagnose and repair just about any software problem — crashes, corrupted files, failed updates, slow systems, startup failures, network drops, and more. It fixes what it can, automatically, and backs up your data before it touches anything.

For hardware, Kinto runs a deep diagnostic across your entire device — your drive, memory, processor, and more — and tells you exactly what it found in plain language, so you can make an informed choice about what to do next.
